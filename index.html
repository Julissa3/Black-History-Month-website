Dr. Kyla McMullen

Dr. Kyla McMullen is a Associate Professor in the University of Michigan. She is also the first African American woman to earn a Ph.D. in Computer Science. Her main focus on audio computing and making STEM more accessible to underrepresented groups.

Accomplishyments
The first African American woman to earn a Ph.D. in Computer Science
Won the NSF Early Career Award in 2019
Won the MUSES and GradSWE Trailblazer Award
Won the National Black Data Processing Associates Professional Achievement Award
